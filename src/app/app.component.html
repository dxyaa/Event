Intro to Fresher's 24
Welcome to the most anticipated event of the year,Freshers Day 24!
We are thrilled to announce this unforgettable gathering that
promises to be a perfect fusion of fun, technology, challenge, and entertainment.
We believe in creating an experience that transcends the ordinaryoffering a delightful blend of workshops, games, and concerts,
all aimed at welcoming and celebrating the newest additions to our community.

Our mission is simple: to provide an immersive platform where fresh faces can forge new friendships,
sharpen their technical skills, embrace exciting challenges, and lose themselves in the euphoria of live music.

From hands-on workshops that empower you with valuable knowledge
to exhilarating games that test your wit and teamwork,
from electrifying concerts that will have you dancing till the dawn
to the intellectual stimulation of technical competitions,
our Offline Freshers Day Event is a celebration like no other.

So, get ready to embark on an unforgettable journey with us,
as we kickstart your college life with a bang, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Events
CIVIL

The civil events at our Offline Freshers Day Event are an embodiment of innovation and problem-solving in the world of civil engineering. From evaluation of case studies to fun games like bridge making and town planning,the events promote creativity and teamwork with excitement and fun.

MECHANICAL

Our mechanical events at the Offline Freshers Day Event are a celebration of innovation and engineering excellence. From 3D printing to model aircraft designing and everything in between,these mechanical events not only offer a hands-on experience but also a platform for participants to develop their innovative thinking.

COMPUTER

The computer events at our Offline Freshers Day Event are a playground for tech enthusiasts and aspiring developers alike. From game development and testing to simple games like typing speed test,these events not only foster technical proficiency but also ignite creativity and competitiveness with thrill and fun.

ELECTRICAL & ELECTRONICS

Our electronic and electrical events promise to be an electrifying experience. From building robotic arms to delving into the field of IoT,these events blend learning and fun, allowing everyone to explore cutting-edge concepts while fostering creativity and technical skills.

GENERAL

Fun events that incorporate workshops and games offer a dynamic and engaging experience for participants. Escape rooms,dominos and puzzles are just a start to the wide range of fun activities offered. These events are not only entertaining but also foster camaraderie, intellectual stimulation, and healthy competition, making them ideal choices for social gatherings and team-building exercises.

Arts
Dance

Freshers' Day events are known for their diverse dance performances. Seniors showcase their experience with traditional, contemporary, or fusion styles, while freshers bring youthful energy and creativity. External dance groups may join, adding variety. These performances foster inclusivity, bringing students of all backgrounds together to celebrate the universal language of dance.

Music

Freshers' Day music events offer a diverse range of musical experiences, from concerts by prominent artists to performances by freshers. These events showcase the talents of both seasoned professionals and budding musicians, creating a sense of unity and celebration within the campus community. They bridge generational gaps and may include music workshops to further develop musical skills. Overall, Freshers' Day music events provide a platform for artists of all levels to connect and share their passion for music.

Creativity

Freshers' Day events highlight creativity through drawing and art displays. Seniors exhibit their skilled work, while freshers bring innovative and raw talent to the forefront. Occasionally, external artists contribute to the diversity of the event. These creative activities encourage inclusivity, bringing students of all backgrounds and skill levels together to celebrate art and imagination.
